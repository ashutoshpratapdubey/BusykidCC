Please review this Direct Notice of Privacy Practices for important information about how we collect, use, and disclose personal information from and about your child. If you do not consent to this Direct Notice, we will not collect, use, or disclose any personal information from your child.

Information We Collect About Children. We collect information about children primarily from you, as well as automatically through your child’s use of our Site, App, or services. We may combine information automatically collected with other information that we have collected about children. In order to create a child’s account, you must provide us with your child’s username, password, age and/or date of birth, and gender. While you provide us with personal information about your child, we will also collect information about your child’s activities within our Site or App in such a way that we are able to associate your child’s activities with his/her username or other identifier, such as an Internet protocol (IP) address. For more information about the information we collect automatically about users, please see the Information We Collect section in our Privacy Policy.

How We Use and Disclose Your Child’s Personal Information. We do not sell children’s personal information and children are not enabled to make their personal information publicly available. In general, we may disclose children’s personal information to provide our Services, to comply with the law, and to protect LeapSpring and other users of our services. For more information about how we use and disclose your child’s personal information, please see the How We Use Your Information and How We Disclose Your Information sections in our Privacy Policy.

Your Rights to Review, Delete, and Control Our Use of Your Child’s Personal Information. You have a right to review your child’s personal information, to delete it, and to tell us to no longer use it. You can review most of the personal information we collect about your child by logging in to your account, reviewing your account information, and updating your child’s personal information. If you do not want us to collect and use your child’s personal information anymore, then you may cancel your child’s account and your child’s use of our Site, App, and services. You may also contact us at the contact information provided above to request access to your child’s information or cancellation of your child’s account. Please note that copies of information that you update, modify, or delete may remain viewable in cached or archived pages for a period of time.

For more information about LeapSpring’s privacy practices general, please see our Privacy Policy. For more information about COPPA and your child’s rights to online privacy, visit http://www.coppa.org/comply.htm.
