Payments

BusyKid uses advanced technology that allows parents to pay allowance by text approval. Each week you will receive a text from BusyKid alerting you how much allowance has been earned and asking your approval. A simple “yes” reply from you and we will move that amount from your linked bank account into your kids BusyKid account.

BusyKid also makes it easy to pay for our service. After your 30 trial is over we will automatically withdraw $12 from your linked checking account to pay for your annual membership. You can cancel this any time.
